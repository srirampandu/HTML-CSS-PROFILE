Shreyas Iyer

Shreyas Iyer is an Indian Cricketer who plays for the National side in international cricket, Mumbai in domestic cricket, and captains Kolkata Knight Riders in the Indian Premier League. A Right Handed top ordered batsman, Iyer has played One Day Internationals, Twenty20 Internationals and Test cricket for the India national cricket team.
